We are a community of students with the goal of supporting Vassar students with marginalized identities. We are here to establish a space for solidarity among first year students of all identities and backgrounds. We believe that what affects one individual on this campus affects us all. We believe that it is through healing, solidarity, accountability, and action that we can create a respectful, articulate, and engaged student body. Through collaboration, we will take action towards making Vassar a more socially conscious and inclusive campus.
